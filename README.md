## What this is

A training portal for data management, built with MkDocs Material. Covers Data Fundamentals, Systems Architecture (SoE / SoR / SoRef), Modern Data Sources, and the rest of the lifecycle — in long-form text rather than a SaaS surface.

Authored by Raja Shahnawaz Soni. Free for personal study and reference.

## Why it still exists

It's not abandoned and it's not duplicate work. It's the **text-first version** of the same curriculum that now powers The MDM Lab's interactive modules. Different shipping format for different reading modes:

| Surface | Best for |
|---|---|
| **The MDM Lab** ([live](https://data-alchemist.raja-cloudmdm.workers.dev)) | "I want to practise, not read" — interactive lessons, AI tutor, workbenches, DPR Lab |
| **This portal** (here) | "I want to read straight through" — long-form chapters, searchable, code-blocky, archive-friendly |

The MkDocs site is **superseded** as the *primary* learning surface, but it's not deprecated as a *reference*. Think of it as the textbook that sits next to the lab.
